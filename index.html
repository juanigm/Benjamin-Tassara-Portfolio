ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Benjamin, I'm a self-taught Video Editing and Motion Graphics student with over 2+ years of experience using Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, and Adobe Photoshop. During my journey I worked creating videos for the Social Networks of an eSports team, for different YouTube channels and some Motion Graphics projects for multiple clients on freelance platforms. Currently, I’m working as a Video Editor and Thumbnail Designer for Matthew Farrahi's YouTube channel.

I consider myself a creative, friendly, responsible and proactive person. I'm a highly detailed person and I always try not to miss a single pixel of error. My goal is to deliver high quality work to the client and with that in mind I work every day to improve.
